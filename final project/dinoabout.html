About Dinosaur Bar-B-Que

Dinosaur Bar-B-Que is a well-known barbecue restaurant located in Syracuse, New York. Since its establishment in 1988, the restaurant has been serving up delicious and authentic barbecue dishes, earning a reputation as a local favorite.
