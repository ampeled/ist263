Riley's - Syracuse

About Riley's

Riley's is a neighborhood eatery with a friendly, cozy ambiance located in Syracuse, New York. Known for its laid-back atmosphere, Riley's serves a range of American staples, making it an ideal spot for a relaxed night out. Guests can enjoy delicious burgers, sandwiches, and the locally famous Chicken Riggies, which have become a favorite among the locals.
