1. Dinosaur Bar-B-Que

A well-known establishment in Syracuse that has been providing delicious barbecue since 1988. Well-known for its soft, smokey meats, this well-liked restaurant is the ideal place for a laid-back evening with loved ones. Try their house-made sides like mac & cheese and simmered greens, as well as their award-winning pulled pork.

2. Pastabilities

A beloved restaurant in the area that serves inventively combined Italian and American fare. Their duck bacon flatbread is a must-try, and they make their handcrafted pasta dishes fresh every day. Remember to try their well-known Spicy Hot Tomato Oil, which is excellent for drizzling on warm, crusty bread.

3. Strong Hearts Café

A vegan haven, Strong Hearts Café serves a variety of mouthwatering plant-based meals. Everyone can find something they enjoy at this quaint restaurant, which serves everything from filling soups to delectable desserts. You must try their legendary milkshakes and the Earth Crisis sandwich.

4. Lemon Grass

Lemon Grass has been providing traditional Thai food with an emphasis on high-quality, fresh ingredients for more than 25 years. The refined atmosphere and attentive service render it a superb choice for a memorable evening. Try their Panang Curry, Pad Thai, and Pla Lad Prik, which is a crispy whole-fried fish served with hot tamarind sauce.

5. The York

The York is a NYC-influenced industrial-chic restaurant and cocktail lounge set in the heart of Syracuse’s historic Armory Square. With the fusion of escapist big-city dining and nightlife feel with fresh renditions of classic seafood preparations and world-class raw bar, The York offers a truly unique and memorable downtown Syracuse dining experience.

6. Riley's

A neighborhood eatery with a friendly, cozy ambiance. This place is ideal for a laid-back night out since it serves a range of American staples. Try their delicious burgers and sandwiches, or their famous Chicken Riggies, which are a local favorite.

7. Eva’s European Sweets

Specializing in traditional Polish food, Eva's European Sweets provides a warm and welcoming dining atmosphere. There are many filling options on the menu, including kielbasa, pierogi, and placki ziemniaczane (potato pancakes). They have an amazing range of house-made cakes and pastries, so be sure to save space for dessert.
