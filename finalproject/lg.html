Lemon Grass - Syracuse

About Lemon Grass

Lemon Grass has been a staple in Syracuse, New York, offering traditional Thai food with an emphasis on high-quality, fresh ingredients for more than 25 years. With its refined atmosphere and attentive service, Lemon Grass provides a superb choice for a memorable evening. Guests can enjoy a diverse menu featuring dishes such as Panang Curry, Pad Thai, and Pla Lad Prik, a crispy whole-fried fish served with hot tamarind sauce.
