Eva's European Sweets - Syracuse

About Eva's European Sweets

Eva's European Sweets is a charming restaurant specializing in traditional Polish food, providing a warm and welcoming dining atmosphere in Syracuse, New York. With a menu featuring filling options like kielbasa, pierogi, and placki ziemniaczane (potato pancakes), Eva's European Sweets is a delightful destination for those craving authentic European flavors. The restaurant also boasts an amazing range of house-made cakes and pastries, making it a sweet ending to any meal.
