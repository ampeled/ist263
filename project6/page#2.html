Popular Syracuse Restaurants

Pastabilities

Pastabilities in Syracuse NY and it was fantastic. The pasta was very tasty and they had many different options. I would recommend the spicy vodka rigatoni as it was my favorite dish. The service was great and the atmosphere was welcoming. I highly recommend trying their pasta if you're in the area.

Faegan's Cafe & Pub

Faegan's Cafe & Pub in Syracuse offers a lively atmosphere and friendly staff perfect for a fun night out. Their pub burger is a must-try. Whether you're a local or passing through it's a great spot for good food and drinks with friends. Don't miss it!

Rachel's

Rachel's at the Sheraton Syracuse offers an elegant dining experience with diverse menu options. The service is excellent, making it a great choice for special occasions. I can't wait to return!
